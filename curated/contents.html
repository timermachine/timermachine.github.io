Kaizen

Kaizen is a term that comes from Zen, meaning continuous improvement.

Perfect for building a good habit. Say you want to Meditate at 8pm each day. Its good to start small - say for two minutes. When you complete the timer, it will increase the duration for the next time. Here by 30 seconds. So the next day you will meditate for 2minutes and 30 seconds. How easy is that! Kaizen makes it effortless to build habits you really want to develop (but have resistance to). It really is mystical magic - genius in it's simplicity. Those Zen Monk dudes know what they were doing!
